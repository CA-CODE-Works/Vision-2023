Our Strategy for Getting to Vision 2023

To achieve Vision 2023, we must understand what’s standing our way.

To inspire leaders and teams to collaborate across government, we have highlighted key challenges faced by each goal. These challenges are intended to help us to set the specific objectives and results for us to achieve each year.

This work is foundational by nature, and not necessarily the shiniest or most newfangled objects. There are not many buzzwords of bleeding-edge technologies in them. However, these are specific challenges that we are actively choosing to address as we have found that we need to establish some fundamentals, build momentum, alignment and trust, surface the clear problems to solve, and prioritize fundamental set-up as we emerge from 2020, a year of reactive response.
