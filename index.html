Vision 2023

Vision 2023 is California’s Statewide Strategic Plan to use technology to meet our society’s goals, and make progress on the big, complex problems affecting us all.

Our plan lays out how technology can power not just more efficient and effective government, but a compassionate and fair government. Our vision is of a government that is accessible and reliable, and works without surprises or hidden traps.
