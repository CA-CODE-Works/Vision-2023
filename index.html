Vision 2023

Vision 2023 is California’s Statewide Strategic Plan for the efficient and effective use of technology to meet our society’s goals on big problems that affect us all.

Vision 2023 tells the story of a California using technology for a government that is compassionate, human-centered, and understanding; a government that works, because more efficient and more effective isn’t good enough. Read the letter from the State CIO.
